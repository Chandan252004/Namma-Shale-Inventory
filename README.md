# Namma-Shaale Inventory – Android App Development using GenAI

##  Project Overview

Namma-Shaale Inventory is a smart Android-based inventory and asset management application designed for primary and secondary schools. The application helps schools digitally track government-funded resources such as sports kits, lab equipment, tablets, and classroom assets.

The app acts as a **Digital Asset Auditor**, enabling teachers and school authorities to efficiently manage, monitor, and maintain school resources.

---
##  Problem Statement

Schools receive valuable educational resources from government programs, but there is no proper digital system to monitor the condition and availability of these assets. As a result:

* Items may get damaged or lost
* Repairs are delayed
* No centralized tracking exists

Namma-Shaale Inventory solves this issue through a simplified digital asset monitoring system.

---

##  Objectives

* Improve accountability in schools
* Ensure proper usage of educational resources
* Simplify asset auditing
* Reduce asset loss and misuse
* Promote digital inventory management

---

##  Features

###  Asset Registration

* Add new school assets
* Store:

  * Asset Name
  * Serial Number
  * Category
  * Asset Photo
  * Purchase Details

###  Monthly Health Check

* Update asset condition:

  * 🟢 Working
  * 🟡 Needs Repair
  * 🔴 Broken
* Maintain maintenance history

###  Issue Tracking

* Record issues such as:

  * Lost items
  * Damaged equipment
  * Missing sports kits
* Add date and remarks

###  Repair Request Management

* Generate repair requests
* Track pending repairs
* View repair history

###  Dashboard & Reports

* Total Assets Count
* Assets Needing Repair
* Asset Status Summary
* Shareable inventory reports
